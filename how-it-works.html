How it works

From application to funds in four simple steps

1

Check your eligibility

Tell us your loan amount, term and a few basic details. We run a soft search that's invisible to other lenders and never affects your credit score, then show you a personalised, no-obligation quote.

2

Choose your amount & term

Use our sliders to fine-tune how much you borrow and over how long. Watch your monthly repayment update live so you can find the balance that fits your budget.

3

Apply online

Complete your full application in around five minutes. We'll ask for identity, income and address details — most customers have everything they need on their phone already.

4

Receive your money

Once approved and you've digitally signed your agreement, funds are typically sent the same or next working day, straight into your nominated bank account.

After you apply

What happens next

Within 90 seconds

Instant decision

Most applications receive an automated decision immediately. More complex applications may take up to one working day for manual review.

Same day

Digital agreement

Review your credit agreement online — clearly laid out with your APR, term and total repayable amount — and sign electronically.

Next working day

Funds released

Once signed, we release funds to your nominated account. Some approvals before 2pm qualify for same-day payment.

Every month

Fixed repayments

Your Direct Debit collects the same fixed amount each month for the life of the loan — no surprises, no variable rates.

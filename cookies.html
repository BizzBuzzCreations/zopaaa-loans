Cookie Policy

Last updated: 1 June 2026. This policy explains what cookies are, which ones zopaaa-loans uses, and how you can control them.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site function correctly, remember your preferences, and give us insight into how the site is used.

2. Types of cookies we use

CategoryPurposeDuration
EssentialRequired for core site functionality, such as remembering your theme preference and cookie consent choice.1 year
PerformanceHelp us understand how visitors use the site so we can improve page speed and content.26 months
FunctionalRemember choices you make, such as calculator inputs, to improve your next visit.6 months
TargetingUsed to measure the effectiveness of marketing campaigns, only with your consent.12 months

3. Managing your preferences

You can change your cookie preferences at any time using the button below, or by adjusting your browser settings to block or delete cookies. Note that blocking essential cookies may affect site functionality.

4. Third-party cookies

Some cookies are set by trusted third parties who provide analytics and security services on our behalf. We only permit third parties to use cookies in line with our data protection standards.

5. Changes to this policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically to reflect changes in technology or regulation. Please check back for the latest version.

6. Contact us

Questions about our use of cookies can be sent to privacy@zopaaa-loans.co.uk.

How we calculate your numbers

We use the standard reducing-balance formula lenders use for fixed-rate personal loans: monthly interest is charged only on your remaining balance, so as you repay, more of each instalment goes towards the principal. Adding extra monthly payments accelerates this, reducing your total interest and the time it takes to become debt-free.

About zopaaa-loans

Lending, the way it should have always worked

We started zopaaa-loans because borrowing shouldn't feel like reading the small print in a foreign language. Fifteen years later, that's still the whole point.

Our story

Founded on one frustration


In 2011, our founders were turned down for a loan with no explanation, then approved elsewhere at three times the advertised rate. zopaaa-loans was built to close that gap between what lenders promise and what borrowers actually experience.

Today we've helped more than 98,500 customers borrow with clarity — from first car purchases to full debt consolidation plans — and we still measure success by how few questions customers need to ask after they've signed.

Our mission

To give every borrower a fair, transparent decision in minutes — with pricing they can understand and terms they can trust, regardless of where they're starting from financially.

Our vision

A lending market where representative examples reflect what most people actually pay, and where "quick and easy" never means "read this later and regret it."

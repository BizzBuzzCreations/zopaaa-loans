Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 June 2026. This policy explains how zopaaa-loans Financial Services Ltd ("zopaaa-loans", "we", "us") collects, uses and protects your personal data when you use our website or apply for a loan.

1. Who we are

zopaaa-loans Financial Services Ltd is registered in England & Wales (Company No. 09876543), with registered office at 1 Ledger Court, Manchester, M1 4AB. We are the data controller for the personal information described in this policy.

2. Information we collect

  • Identity data: name, date of birth, and identification documents.
  • Contact data: address, email address and phone numbers.
  • Financial data: income, employment status, bank details and credit history obtained from credit reference agencies.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device information and pages visited on our website.
  • Usage data: how you interact with our calculators, forms and account tools.

3. How we use your information

  • To assess your eligibility and process loan applications.
  • To carry out identity verification and fraud prevention checks.
  • To manage your loan account and communicate about repayments.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations, including those set by the Financial Conduct Authority.
  • To improve our website and services through aggregated, anonymised analytics.
  • To send marketing communications where you've opted in, which you can unsubscribe from at any time.

4. Legal basis for processing

We process your data under the following legal bases: performance of a contract (processing your loan application and agreement), legal obligation (regulatory reporting and anti-money laundering checks), legitimate interests (fraud prevention and service improvement), and consent (marketing communications and non-essential cookies).

5. Sharing your information

We may share data with credit reference agencies, fraud prevention agencies, regulators such as the FCA, IT and cloud service providers acting under contract, and lending partners where we act as a broker. We never sell your personal data to third parties for their own marketing purposes.

6. International transfers

Where any supplier processes data outside the UK, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as UK International Data Transfer Agreements or adequacy decisions.

7. Data retention

We retain application and loan account data for six years after the relationship ends, in line with FCA record-keeping requirements. Website analytics data is retained for no longer than 26 months.

8. Your rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request erasure, where we have no overriding legal obligation to retain it.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing, including direct marketing.
  • Request a portable copy of data you've provided to us.
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).

To exercise any of these rights, contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@zopaaa-loans.co.uk.

9. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to operate securely, remember your preferences and understand how visitors use our site. See our Cookie Policy for full details and how to manage your preferences.

10. Children's privacy

Our services are intended for individuals aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 18.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be notified via our website or by email where appropriate.

12. Contact us

Questions about this policy can be sent to privacy@zopaaa-loans.co.uk or via our Contact page.
